Returns & Exchanges

30-Day Return Policy

We want you to love your DIGINESS products. If you're not completely satisfied, you can return any item within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • All accessories and documentation must be included
  • Products must not show signs of wear or damage
  • Original receipt or proof of purchase required

How to Return

  • Log into your account and initiate a return under "Order History"
  • Print the prepaid return label (domestic returns only)
  • Pack the item securely in original packaging
  • Drop off at any authorized shipping location
  • Keep the tracking number until refund is processed

Refund Timeline

Step Timeframe
Return received at warehouse 3-7 business days
Inspection & processing 2-3 business days
Refund issued to payment method Immediate after approval
Reflects in your account 3-10 business days (depends on bank)
Non-returnable items: Gift cards, software downloads, and personalized items cannot be returned.

Warranty

2-Year Limited Warranty

All DIGINESS products come with a 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects and hardware malfunctions. This includes:

  • Battery defects (normal degradation excluded)
  • Charging port failures
  • Button or sensor malfunctions
  • Display issues
  • Connectivity hardware problems

Not covered: Physical damage, water damage (unless specified IP rating is violated), normal wear and tear, lost items, or unauthorized repairs.

Exchanges

Need a different size, color, or model? Exchanges are processed as a return and new purchase for fastest service.

  • Return the original item using the return process above
  • Place a new order for the desired item
  • Refund will be processed once return is received

International Returns

For customers outside India:

  • International returns are customer responsible for shipping costs
  • We recommend using a tracked and insured shipping service
  • Please mark the package as "Returned Goods" to avoid customs charges
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable
  • Customs fees and duties paid are non-refundable

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged or with incorrect items:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage and packaging
  • We'll arrange a replacement or refund immediately
  • Return shipping for damaged items is always free

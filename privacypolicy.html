Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 28, 2026

Introduction

At DIGINESS, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or make purchases from our store. Please read this policy carefully. By accessing or using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

We are committed to protecting your personal data and being transparent about what we collect and why. If you have any questions, you can always contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@diginess.com.

Information We Collect

Personal Identifiers

Name, email address, phone number, shipping/billing address, account credentials.

Payment Information

Credit/debit card details (processed securely via PCI-compliant partners), UPI IDs, payment history.

Device & Usage

IP address, browser type, device identifiers, pages visited, time spent, clicks, and referral sources.

Order History

Products purchased, returns, exchanges, warranties, and customer support interactions.

Sensitive Data

We do not intentionally collect sensitive information such as biometric data, health information, or political opinions. Any such data you provide inadvertently will be deleted immediately upon discovery.

How We Collect Information

  • Direct interactions: Account registration, orders, newsletter sign-ups, contact forms, and customer support.
  • Automated technologies: Cookies, log files, web beacons, and analytics tools when you browse our site.
  • Third parties: Payment processors, shipping carriers, and fraud prevention services (only as necessary).
  • Social media: If you interact with our social media accounts, we may receive profile information.

How We Use Your Information

Purpose Legal Basis
Process and fulfill orders Contract performance
Communicate about orders, returns, or support Contract performance / Legitimate interest
Send marketing communications (with consent) Consent
Improve website and personalize experience Legitimate interest
Fraud detection and security Legal obligation / Legitimate interest

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies. You'll see a cookie banner on your first visit allowing you to customize preferences.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential: Required for site functionality (cart, login, checkout).
  • Analytics: Help us understand how visitors use our site (Google Analytics, Firebase).
  • Marketing: Track ad effectiveness and deliver relevant promotions.
  • Functional: Remember your preferences and settings.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We never sell your personal information. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Shipping carriers (Delhivery, FedEx, DHL), payment gateways (Razorpay, Stripe), and email/SMS platforms.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect rights and safety.
  • Business transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale (with notice to you).
  • With your consent: Any other purpose you explicitly authorize.
All third-party providers are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for the specified services.

International Data Transfers

We are based in India but may transfer data to servers or service providers in other countries (e.g., USA, EU, Singapore) for processing. These countries may have different data protection laws. When we transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:

  • Standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission
  • Binding corporate rules where applicable
  • Data localization for sensitive Indian customer data (as required by Indian law)

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Data Type Retention Period
Account information Until account deletion + 90 days backup
Order history 7 years (tax/legal requirements)
Marketing communications Until unsubscribe + 30 days
Website analytics 26 months (Google Analytics policy)

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

Access

Request a copy of your personal data we hold.

Deletion

Request deletion of your data (subject to legal limits).

Correction

Update inaccurate or incomplete information.

Opt-out

Withdraw consent for marketing or certain processing.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@diginess.com. We will respond within 30 days (faster wherever possible).

For Indian users: Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, you have additional rights to grievance redressal and data portability. You can also contact our Data Protection Officer directly.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and multi-factor authentication for employees
  • PCI-DSS compliant payment processing
  • Anonymization and pseudonymization where feasible

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication on your account.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. For significant changes, we may also send an email notification.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
